## Quality Standards

Each word must have:
1. **Compelling hook** — One sentence that creates curiosity
2. **4+ paragraph story** — Journey through time and place
3. **5+ journey stops** — Geographic progression with coordinates
4. **3+ sounds** — Original, intermediate, modern pronunciations
5. **3+ relatives** — At least one linking to existing words
6. **Strong meaningNow** — Why this word matters today

## Content Pipeline

1. **Research**: 2-3 hours per word (etymology sources, historical context)
2. **Draft**: 1 hour (story, journey mapping)
3. **Audio**: Source Forvo pronunciations or TTS
4. **Review**: Cross-check etymology claims
5. **Coordinate mapping**: Verify historical locations
